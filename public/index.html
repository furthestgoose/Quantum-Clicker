Quantum Clicker

Quantum Clicker is an engaging idle game developed in Swift that takes you on a journey from classical computing to the quantum realm. Progress through various stages of technological advancement, unlock upgrades, and dive into the fascinating world of quantum computing!

Features

  • Over 100 unique upgrades to enhance your progress
  • 27 different computer types to purchase and upgrade
  • Achievement system to track your accomplishments
  • Prestige system with special prestige upgrades
  • Engaging idle gameplay mechanics

Installation

As this game is not currently available on the App Store, you'll need to build the project in Xcode yourself. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Clone this repository to your local machine.
  2. Open the project in Xcode.
  3. Ensure you have the latest version of Xcode installed.
  4. Build and run the project on your iOS device or simulator.

Requirements

  • Xcode 13.0 or later
  • iOS 15.0 or later
  • Swift 5.5 or later

How To Play

  1. Start with a basic classical computer and generate computing power.
  2. Use your computing power to purchase upgrades and new computer types.
  3. Progress through different eras of computing technology.
  4. Unlock achievements as you reach milestones.
  5. Prestige when you're ready to boost your progress and unlock special upgrades.
